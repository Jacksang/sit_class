## Open Questions for Discussion

### Conceptual Questions
1. **69/31 vs. 50/50:** Is there ever a "right" equity split for student teams, or does it always depend on specific circumstances?

2. **Team charter enforcement:** What happens when someone signs the charter but doesn't follow through? Is it enforceable without legal backing?

3. **Classic conflict:** If two team members want to go in different strategic directions (e.g., build vs. sell), how do you resolve it?

### Application Questions
4. **Your team charter:** What was the most difficult section to agree on? Why?

5. **Conflict resolution:** Have you had a team disagreement yet? How did you handle it — and would you handle it differently now?

6. **Role alignment:** Do you feel like you're in the right role on your team? If you could switch, would you?

### Singapore Context Questions
7. In Singapore, many students know each other from junior college or polytechnic. Does this prior relationship help or hurt when forming startup teams?

8. Singapore has strong IP protection laws. But for student projects, how concerned should you be about someone "stealing" your idea?
