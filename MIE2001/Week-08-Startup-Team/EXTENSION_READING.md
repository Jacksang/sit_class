## Enrichment Readings

### Founder Dynamics
1. **"Slicing Pie: How to Split Equity in a Startup"** — Mike Moyer
   - The definitive guide to dynamic equity splitting
   - Ch 1-3: Principles of "fair equity for all"

2. **"The Founder's Dilemma"** — Noam Wasserman (HBR, 2008)
   - Why most founders are replaced as CEO
   - The trade-off between control and wealth
   - Rich vs. King: what do you want more?

3. **"How to Choose a Co-Founder"** — Y Combinator (blog)
   - Practical advice: look for complementary skills, shared values, and conflict resolution ability
   - Interview questions to ask potential co-founders

### Team Management
4. **"The Five Dysfunctions of a Team"** — Patrick Lencioni (Ch 1-3)
   - Absence of trust → Fear of conflict → Lack of commitment → Avoidance of accountability → Inattention to results
   - Useful diagnostic for team issues

5. **"Radical Candor"** — Kim Scott (Ch 1)
   - How to give feedback that's both caring and direct
   - "Care personally" + "Challenge directly"

### Ethics & Startup Governance
6. **"The Startup Ethics Handbook"** — Harvard Business Review
   - Practical scenarios for early-stage ethical decisions
   - Data privacy, honest marketing, equitable treatment

7. **"Singapore Code of Corporate Governance"** — Monetary Authority of Singapore
   - The official governance framework (advanced reading)
   - Useful context for students interested in the regulatory side
