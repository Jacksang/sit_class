# Week 7 — Extension Reading & Enrichment

## Pre-Pitch Prep Reading

**"The Art of the Start 2.0"** — Guy Kawasaki (Ch 4: "The Art of Pitches")
- The classic 10/20/30 rule (10 slides, 20 min, 30 pt font) — adapted for our 5-min format
- How to tell a compelling story about your business

**"How to Give a 5-Minute Pitch"** — Y Combinator Startup School (YouTube, 12 min)
- Practical advice on time management, storytelling, and Q&A handling

## Post-Pitch Reflection

**"What I Learned From My First Failed Startup"** — Blog by a Singapore founder
- Normalizes the experience of mid-term pivots
- Good reading after the mid-term reality check
