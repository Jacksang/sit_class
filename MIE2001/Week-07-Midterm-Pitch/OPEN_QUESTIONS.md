## Open Questions After Mid-Term

### Reflection Questions
1. **Post-pitch clarity:** After hearing other teams' pitches, would you change anything about your approach?

2. **Critical feedback:** What's the hardest piece of feedback you received today? Were they right?

3. **Team dynamics:** After working together for 4 weeks, what's working well? What's challenging?

### Looking Ahead
4. **Pivot decision:** Based on your MVP test (if done), do you need to pivot your idea or problem statement?

5. **Help needed:** What's the one thing you most need help with right now? (Who in this room could help?)
