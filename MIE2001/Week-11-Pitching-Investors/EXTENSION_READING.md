# Week 11 — Extension Reading & Enrichment

## Core Reading (Required)

**"The Art of the Start 2.0"** — Guy Kawasaki (Ch 4: "The Art of Pitches")
- 10/20/30 rule explained
- How to create a compelling pitch narrative

## Enrichment Readings

### Pitch Deck Design
1. **"Pitch Anything"** — Oren Klaff (Ch 1-3)
   - The neuroscience of pitching: how to engage the "croc brain"
   - Frame control, status, and novelty in presentations

2. **"The Nail: Being the Only One Who Gets It"** — Jason Fried (blog)
   - Simple, memorable advice on pitching
   - "You need one big idea, not lots of little ones"

3. **"The Best Pitch Decks in the World"** — Hustle Fund / PitchDeckHunt
   - Real pitch decks from funded startups
   - Analyze: what do they all have in common?

### Storytelling
4. **"Made to Stick"** — Chip & Dan Heath (Ch on stories)
   - Why stories work better than data
   - The SUCCESs model: Simple, Unexpected, Concrete, Credible, Emotional, Story

5. **"Storytelling for Startups"** — Y Combinator (blog)
   - How to find your startup's narrative
   - The "before/after" bridge

### Singapore Pitch Resources
6. **"SLINGSHOT Pitch Competition"** — EnterpriseSG
   - SG's biggest startup competition
   - Watch past winners' pitches on YouTube

7. **"SIT Entrepreneurship Competition"** — SIT I&E Office
   - Local competition with pitch guidelines and examples
