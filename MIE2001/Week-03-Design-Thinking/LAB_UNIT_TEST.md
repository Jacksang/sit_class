*Conducted during class as the main activity — see PPT outline for the full sprint flow*

### Sprint Recap (for instructor reference)

| Stage | Time | Activity |
|-------|------|----------|
| Empathize | 10 min | Campus observation walk |
| Define | 5 min | Write 3 HMW statements |
| Ideate | 10 min | Brainstorm 15+ solutions |
| Prototype | 15 min | Low-fidelity sketch/script |
| Test | 10 min | Share with another group |
| Debrief | 10 min | Group share-out |
