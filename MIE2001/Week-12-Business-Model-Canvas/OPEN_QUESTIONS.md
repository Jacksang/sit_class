## Open Questions for Reflection

### Final Reflection Questions
1. **From Week 1 to now:** Rate your confidence as an entrepreneur on a scale of 1-10. How has it changed since Week 1?

2. **Biggest lesson:** What's the single most important thing you learned this semester — about entrepreneurship, about yourself, or about working in a team?

3. **What you'd do differently:** If you could restart the semester, what would you change?

4. **What you'd keep:** What did you do well that you'd repeat?

### Forward-Looking Questions
5. **After this module:** Will you continue working on this venture? Start a new one? Take a break from entrepreneurship?

6. **Pivot or persevere:** Is your Week 1 idea still your Week 13 idea? If not, what changed?
