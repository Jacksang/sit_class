# Week 12 — Extension Reading & Enrichment

## Core Reading (Required)

**"Business Model Generation"** — Alexander Osterwalder & Yves Pigneur (Ch 1-2)
- The definitive guide to the Business Model Canvas
- Visual examples from real companies

## Enrichment Readings

### BMC in Depth
1. **"Value Proposition Design"** — Osterwalder et al.
   - The companion book to Business Model Generation
   - Deep dive on the Value Proposition Canvas
   - Practical worksheets and examples

2. **"The Invincible Company"** — Osterwalder et al.
   - How to manage business model portfolios
   - Innovation portfolio, culture, and metrics

3. **"Business Model You"** — Tim Clark
   - Applying the BMC framework to YOUR career
   - A fun exercise: what's your personal business model?

### Case Studies
4. **"Grab's Business Model Evolution"** — Case study
   - From taxi dispatch to super-app: how BMC changed over time
   - Good example of iteration

5. **"How Carousell Built Its Business Model"** — Tech in Asia
   - From C2C classifieds to monetized marketplace
   - Revenue model evolution

6. **"10 Singapore Startups and Their Business Models"** — Vulcan Post
   - Quick case studies on SG startups
   - Good reference for comparing BMC approaches

### BMC Design
7. **"The Lean Canvas"** — Ash Maurya
   - Adaptation of BMC for early-stage startups
   - Replaces "Key Partners" with "Unfair Advantage" and "Customer Relationships" with "Solution"
   - Good alternative if BMC feels too big-company-oriented

8. **"Testing Business Models"** — Alexander Osterwalder (blog)
   - How to test each BMC block
   - Experiment design for business model validation

## Smart Brevity Version
If you only have 10 minutes:
1. Read your BMC left-to-right. Does it tell one coherent story?
2. Pick ONE block that feels weakest and do 5 minutes of research on it
