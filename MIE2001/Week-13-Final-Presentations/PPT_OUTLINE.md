| Criteria | Weight | What Judges Evaluate |

| Problem & Validation | 20% | Is the problem real? Evidence from interviews? |

| Business Model | 20% | BMC completeness? Revenue model? Unit economics? |
| Market & Competition | 15% | Market sizing? Competitive analysis? |
| Team & Storytelling | 15% | Is the pitch compelling? Does the team fit? |

### Slide 7 — Closing Remarks
> "This module wasn't about building a perfect company in 13 weeks. It was about learning how to THINK like an entrepreneur. And you've done that. Here's what we've learned together:

> 1. **Entrepreneurship is a method, not a magic trick.** You can learn it. You just did.
> 2. **Problems matter more than solutions.** Stay in love with the problem.
> 3. **Talk to customers.** Your best ideas come from understanding real people.
> 4. **Start small, test fast.** An MVP is a learning vehicle, not a product.
> 5. **Teams beat individuals.** Find people who complement you.

> Whether you continue this venture or start a new one next month, you now have the tools. Go build something."
