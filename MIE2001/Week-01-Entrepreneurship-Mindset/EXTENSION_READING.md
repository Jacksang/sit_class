# Week 1 — Extension Reading & Enrichment

## Core Reading (Required)

**"What Makes Entrepreneurs Entrepreneurial?"** — Saras D. Sarasvathy (HBR, 2001)
- The foundational paper on effectuation theory
- Concise, readable, with vivid examples
- Available via SIT library database or HBR.org

## Enrichment Readings

### Mindset & Identity
1. **"Mindset: The New Psychology of Success"** — Carol Dweck (Ch 1)
   - The full growth vs. fixed mindset framework
   - Practical advice for cultivating growth mindset
   - Library call number: BF773 .D94 2006

2. **"The Entrepreneurial Mindset"** — Rita McGrath & Ian MacMillan (HBR)
   - How established companies think like startups
   - Five principles of the entrepreneurial mindset

### Singapore-Specific Context
3. **"State of the Singapore Startup Ecosystem 2024"** — SGInnovate / NUS Enterprise
   - Latest data on funding, sectors, and trends
   - Good for understanding "what's hot" in SG right now
   - Download from: www.sginnovate.com

4. **"How Grab Started: The Story of Singapore's First Unicorn"** — Tech in Asia
   - Case study of effectuation in action
   - Anthony Tan and Tan Hooi Ling's early days

### Practical Frameworks
5. **"The 5 Most Important Startup Skills"** — Steve Blank (blog)
   - Customer discovery, agile development, business model design
   - Short read, good framing for the semester ahead
