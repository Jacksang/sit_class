# Week 9 — Lab & Unit Test Design

## Lab: Persona Creation & Channel Strategy (50 min)

### Objective
Create a detailed customer persona and select your top 3 acquisition channels.

### Part A: Persona Creation (20 min, teams)

**Step 1:** Gather raw materials (5 min)
- Look at your interview notes
- What patterns do you see across interviewees?
- What's a common behavior or frustration?

**Step 2:** Create your persona (15 min)
- Use the template from the PPT (Slide 10)
- Give them a name, age, and backstory
- Write a quote in their voice
- Draw or describe their face

**Presentation prep (time permitting):**
- Each team: 60 seconds to introduce their persona

### Part B: Channel Brainstorming & Scoring (20 min, teams)

**Step 1:** List 10 channels (3 min)
- Brainstorm every possible channel to reach your persona

**Step 2:** Score each channel (7 min)

| Channel | Reach (1-5) | Relevance (1-5) | Cost (1=high, 5=low) | Score = (R×Rel)/C |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|----------------------|-------------------|
| | | | | |
| | | | | |
| ...10 channels total | | | | |

**Step 3:** Pick your top 3 (5 min)
- Which channels will you test first?
- How will you test them? (specific action, not just "post on Instagram")

**Step 4:** Customer journey map (5 min)
- Draft the journey: Awareness → Consideration → Purchase → Retention → Referral
- What happens at each stage?
